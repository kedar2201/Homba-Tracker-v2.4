Radar User Manual

Control & Customization Guide

V1.0 - USER READY
Welcome to the Radar Control Center. This guide explains which settings you can modify to tune the "Intelligence" of your stock monitoring system. Use this manual to understand the effect of every toggle and filter.

1 Identification & Targets

Target Price (₹)

Your desired buy price. The system monitors live LTP and shows you how many percentage points you are away from this goal.

2 Technical Filters (Timing Your Entry)

These fields control the "Buying Logic" for specific scrips. Modifying these changes when you get an alert.

DIP Dip Goal (%)

Default: 8%. Change this to 10% or 15% if you only want to buy deep crashes. Reducing it to 5% makes the system more aggressive in looking for minor pullbacks.

RSI RSI Floor

Default: 38. Lowering this to 30 will ensure alerts only trigger when the stock is extremely oversold. Raising it to 45 triggers faster but with higher risk of further falling.

DMA DMA Proximity (%)

Controls how "close" the price needs to be to its 50/200 Day averages. Increase this % if you want to capture stocks that might bounce *before* hitting the average line.

VOL Breakout Toggle

Switch this OFF if you only want to buy on dips. Switch it ON if you want alerts when a stock is breaking a 3-month high with massive volume.

3 Sector Intelligence (Defining Quality)

Choose the right Sector Type to unlock industry-specific health checks. The system will automatically switch which fields it requires.

If you choose... You can modify... What it calculates...
Standard ROE & ROCE Measures efficient use of capital for IT/FMCG.
Banking NIM & GNPA NIM tracks profit margin; GNPA tracks bad loan safety.
Insurance EV Growth & Solvency EV tracks future growth; Solvency tracks claim safety.

4 Custom Strategy Weights

Inside the Add/Edit Scrip form, you can enable "Use Custom Strategy Weight". This is the most powerful feature in the app.

  • The Effect: You can decide that "Dip" is worth 30 points instead of 12 for a specific stock.
  • Balance: Ensure your total points for that specific scrip stay around 100 for accurate scoring.
Pro Tip: Use the "Sync Stats" button (the refresh icon) inside the Edit form to automatically fetch the latest ROE, ROCE, and PE from the market. This saves you from manual data entry!
